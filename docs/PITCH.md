## Slide 1 — The problem

**Onchain alpha is scattered across a dozen tabs, and none of them talk to each other.**

- To judge one narrative you check: the token chart, a Polymarket odds page, crypto Twitter sentiment, a rug scanner, a smart-money tracker, and the news.
- Every tool speaks one language. None tells you where they *disagree* — and disagreement between markets pricing the same story is the actual signal.
- Existing agents are single-domain: a meme scanner, or an odds bot, or a wallet tracker. You still do the synthesis yourself.

> The work isn't getting the data. It's connecting it — and that's an hour you don't have when a narrative is moving.

## Slide 2 — The product

**Optic AI is one agent that reads the whole onchain economy and does the research for you.**

Ask in plain words, get a decisive JSON verdict plus a shareable card in ~15 seconds:

- **"who wins the world cup"** → ranked favourites from live market volume + the form/injury research behind them.
- **"where's the edge today"** → markets where the price looks soft vs. the actual research.
- **paste a token** → 0-100 rug-safety score with the red flags, or its lifecycle timing, or what smart money is doing with it.

One engine — *narrative → attention → per-venue read → divergence → verdict* — applied through six lenses (attention, meme, prediction, research, rug, smart-money). Six services live on the marketplace, priced 0.05–0.5 USDT.

Honest by design: never a fabricated win rate, never a "buy this" instruction. It reports the map, and tracks every pick on-chain.
